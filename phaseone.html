An Introduction

This Phase is dedicated to introducing the characters and movie to the audience (you)

Let's introduce the characters now,

Starting with:

Characters From The Movie

He's Spider-Man!

Also, He's The Main Character!

Born in New York City, he grew up living a normal life until one day, he met Spider-Man fighting. Shortly after, he died. Before Spider-Man died, he gave a flash drive to Miles and gave him a mission. Having watched Spider-Man die, he fled the scene before bumping into... Spider-Man?




He's Also Spider-Man!

Except, He's From Another Dimension!

Although, he's not his spry, 20 year old form, but in fact his 30 year old counterpart! He's much older, slightly less agile and broke up with Mary Jane. He was being lazy in his room (as always) when he was sucked into a portal and went through to Miles' dimension, and shortly after, he met Miles.




She's Spider-Gwen!

Spider-Man, but a woman.

The lady behind the mask for Spider-Gwen is Gwen Stacy, Spider-Man's counterpart from another dimension. She was first introduced to Miles when she tracked him down to a prestigious school he was in and enrolled there.




He's Spider-Ham!

He's A... Pig?

Peter Porker is Spider-Ham! He was a fairly normal spider, until he was bitten by a radioactive pig and became Spider-Ham! He also comes from a different dimension, one where everyone is an animal.




He's Spider-Man Noir!

AKA Peter Parker

Animated totally in black and white, Spider-Man Noir is a Spider-Man with limited spider webbing and mainly sticking to guns, becoming Spider-Man Noir in the year 1933, in the midst of The Great Depression, beating up Nazis.




She's A Little Girl!

Also, From The Year 3145!

Peni Parker is a little anime-schoolgirl-looking person who has a psychic link with a formerly-radioactive-spider after being bitten by it, and now it resides in her father's crime-fighting robot.

All Of These Spider-People Came To Miles' Dimension After Kingpin Used The Collider To Search Different Dimensions To Find A Replacement For His Dead Wife And Kids.



Want To Learn More? Scroll Down To See Stories Of The Villains

He's Kingpin!

AKA Wilson Fisk

Standing at a gargantuan two metres, Wilson Fisk is a rich and powerful crime boss who lost his wife and son, Vanessa and Richard Fisk, when they ran away after seeing him fighting Spider-Man. They were driving away when they were caught off guard by a truck which collided into thir car, killing them both. Kingpin now seeks revenge and a new family, hiring Doc Ock to build the collider, a machine which scans different dimensions for genetic matches of his wife and son.

She's Doctor Octopus!

AKA Olivia Octavius

Olivia Octavius is a genius scientist who built a collider which scours the multiverse for the Kingpin as she believed that that would make her a world-renowned scientist.

He's The Prowler!

A Mysterious Guy In A Suit And Mask

The Prowler, hired by The Kingpin to kill Miles Morales, the "New Spider-Man", is revealed to actually be Miles' Uncle, Uncle Aaron Davis. When Aaron finds out that Miles is the person he's trying to kill, Aaron gently puts Miles' mask back on, failing the task set by Kingpin. As a result, Aaron is shot by Kingpin in the back, and is left dead in an alley, whose body was found by his own bother, police officer Jefferson Davis, Miles' father.
