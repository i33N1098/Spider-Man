The first video is made by a normal YouTube channel but features quotes and tips from the team in various interviews while the other video features the animators behind the movie talking.


Some statistics for the movie:
The movie, at one point, had 177 animators on staff. To put that in retrospect, the original Toy Story had 27 animators.
It took the animators one week to get just one second of footage. usually, it takesd about one week to get 4 seconds for footage.
The animators were so particular about the style of the movie, it took one year to get just ten seconds of footage they were happy with.
The movie has a 2 - 3 times higher shot count than any other animated film.
